About

TwoByTwo is a double exposure photo app.

It is a collaboration app that allows you to create double exposure image with friends (or with a random stranger.) You take a photo and it immediately goes to a waiting list where others can etch a second exposure over it. Equally you are presented with a feed of single exposure photos you can use as a base to etch a second exposure over them. The results are always fun, unexpected and experimental.

TwoByTwo is made with love in NYC. Built by John Tubert, Joseph Lin and Amin Torres.
