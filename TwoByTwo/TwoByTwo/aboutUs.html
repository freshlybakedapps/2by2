About
TwoByTwo is a double exposure photo app.
TwoByTwo is a collaboration app that allows you to create double exposure image with a random stranger.
You take a photo and it immediately goes to a waiting list where other photographers can etch a second exposure over it. Equally you are presented with a feed of single exposure photos you can use as a base to etch a second exposure over them. The results are always fun, unexpected and experimental.

TwoByTwo is made with love in NYC.
Built by John Tubert, Joseph Lin and
